How It Works

RadioFAF uses advanced AI technology to generate live, unscripted debates:

  • Dynamic Generation: Every show is generated live using AI
  • Voice Synthesis: Powered by xAI Grok's realtime voice API
  • Real-time Streaming: WebSocket audio streaming for instant playback
  • Unique Content: No two shows are ever the same
  • Live Debates: Voices react to each other in real-time

Our Philosophy

Authentic AI: We believe AI can be genuine without being human. Our voices have real opinions, make real arguments, and sometimes make real mistakes.

Unscripted: No pre-written content. Every word is generated live based on the topic and conversation flow.

Human Stuff by AI: The messy, unpredictable, passionate debates that make radio compelling—delivered by AI that cares about the topics.
